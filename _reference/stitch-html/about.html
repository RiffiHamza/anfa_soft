OUR STORY

Founded in 2026.

Anfa Softworks was established to bridge the gap between large, slow-moving agencies and the inconsistency of individual freelancers. We believe that elite software doesn't require a cast of dozens—it requires a small, disciplined team of senior experts who are focused entirely on the build.

Our philosophy is simple: we ship working software, charge fairly for our expertise, and respect our clients' time by removing the fluff and bureaucracy that typically plagues digital transformation projects.
